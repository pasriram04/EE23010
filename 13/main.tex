\textbf{Question:} In a dice game, a player pays a stake of Re 1 for each throw of a die. She receives Rs 5 if the die shows a 3, Rs 2 if the die shows a 1 or 6, and nothing otherwise. What is the playerâ€™s expected profit per throw over a long series of throws?.\\
\solution
%\fi
Let the random variable $X$ denote the net profit on the roll of a die.
\begin{table}[h!]
    % Change address in github
    \input{tables/randomvar.tex}

Thus, the probability distribution function of $X$ is:
\begin{align}
    p_X(k) = 
    \begin{cases}
        \frac{3}{6} & \text{if } k = -1\\
        \frac{2}{6} & \text{if } k = 1\\
        \frac{1}{6} & \text{if } k = 4
    \end{cases}
\end{align}\\
The expected profit per roll over a long series of throws, in Rupees, is
\begin{align}
    E\brak{X} &= \sum_{k} k p_X(k) \\
    &= \frac{3}{6}\brak{-1} + \frac{2}{6}\brak{1} + \frac{1}{6}\brak{4}\\
    &= 0.5
\end{align}
